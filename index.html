Why Hyderabad

India's tech capital deserves
its own security conference

India's security research community has grown significantly over the years, with regional conferences playing a key role in connecting practitioners, building local talent, and bringing global expertise closer to home.

Hyderabad has no comparable event — despite being one of India's largest technology employers and home to security teams at nearly every major global company.

HysecCon is built to change that. One day, one track, zero filler — just original research, deep technical talks, and hands-on competition.

1,500+ Global Capability Centers in Hyderabad
Top 3 Indian metros by IT/ITES employment
0 Major technical security conferences hosted in city — until now
