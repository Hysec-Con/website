About HysecCon

A security conference
worthy of Hyderabad.

Technical. Community-led. Community-first. Built to last.

The Hyderabad Security Conference (HysecCon) 2026 is a one-day, deeply technical security conference taking place on 28 November 2026 in Hyderabad. Venue to be announced.

India hosts world-class security conferences — Nullcon in Goa, BSides Ahmedabad, c0c0n in Kerala. Each has helped shape regional research communities. Hyderabad — one of India's largest technology hubs and home to the security teams of nearly every major global company — has had no comparable event. HysecCon exists to change that.

Every talk is selected through an open Call for Papers, reviewed by an independent technical board. There are no vendor pitches, no paid speaking slots, and no sponsored content masquerading as research. The day also features a Capture the Flag competition open to students and professionals across Telangana and beyond.

Our values

What we stand for

💻

100% Technical

No sales pitches. Every talk must demonstrate original research, tooling, or hands-on security work.

🔍

Peer-Reviewed

An open CFP reviewed by an independent board. Selections are made on technical merit alone.

🤝

Community-First

Organised by Hyderabad's security community, for the community. Not a corporate event.

🌐

Open Access

Affordable and accessible — we want every curious practitioner in Hyderabad to be able to attend.

🏆

Hands-On Learning

A live CTF alongside technical talks so attendees don't just hear about security — they practice it.

📅

Built to Recur

Designed as an annual flagship event — long-term value for Hyderabad's security ecosystem.
