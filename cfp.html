Call for Papers

Share your research
with Hyderabad.

HysecCon 2026 is looking for technical talks selected purely on merit — no vendor pitches, no sales, just original security work. CFP closes when slots fill up.

What we're looking for

Talk topics

We welcome any technically rigorous submission. Here are areas of particular interest:

Web application security & API attacks
Binary exploitation & reverse engineering
Cloud & infrastructure security
Mobile security (Android / iOS)
Malware analysis & threat intelligence
Defensive engineering & detection
Hardware & embedded / IoT security
Cryptography & protocol analysis
AI / LLM security & adversarial ML
OSINT & physical security
Incident response & digital forensics
Novel research — any technical topic welcome
Submission Guidelines

Before you submit

1

Original work only. Talks must present your own research or project. Repurposed vendor pitches or purely promotional content will be rejected.

2

Technical depth required. Include a clear abstract (200–400 words) describing the problem, your methodology, and the takeaways for the audience.

3

Talk duration. Standard slots are 30 or 50 minutes including Q&A. Indicate your preferred length.

4

Responsible disclosure. If your research involves active vulnerabilities, ensure you have followed responsible disclosure with the affected vendor before submitting.

5

Review process. All submissions are reviewed by an independent technical board. Decisions are based on novelty, technical depth, and relevance.

6

Speaker benefits. Accepted speakers receive a complimentary conference pass. Travel and accommodation are not covered at this stage.

Ready to submit?

Fill in the form with your talk title, abstract, and a short bio. We'll get back to you with a decision within a few weeks of submission.
